At Alpine Ski Academy, we know that every adult has a unique story with skiing. Some start from scratch, others seek to perfect their technique or regain confidence after a fall, and many simply want to enjoy the mountains more, with total safety and control.

Whatever your starting point, our private ski lessons for adults are tailor-made for you. With a certified instructor by your side, we adapt each session to your level, age, physical condition and objectives.

Our Adult Students in Action

Why Choose a Private Lesson as an Adult in Baqueira?

Teaching an adult to ski is different from teaching a child. It requires sensitivity, active listening, technical precision and a methodology that adapts to each pace and expectation.

100% Personalized Attention

Your instructor is dedicated exclusively to you. We evaluate your initial level, identify possible blocks and together define what you want to achieve.

Visible Technical Progress

We help you understand and improve your basic technique, posture, turn execution and control on descents. You'll notice improvement from the first lesson.

Confidence and Safety

If you have fear after a fall or time without skiing, we work on your confidence with patience, progressive exercises and well-chosen slopes.

Lessons at Your Pace

You choose duration, schedule and meeting point. Total flexibility for your comfort in Baqueira, Beret or Bonaigua.

What Level Are You? We Adapt the Lesson to You

Every adult experiences skiing differently. At Alpine Ski Academy, we adjust each lesson not only to your technical level, but also to your confidence, physical condition and personal goals.

Beginner

Discover Skiing with Safety and Good Feelings

If it's your first time in the snow or it's been years since you skied, we start with the essentials: balance, gliding, braking and wedge turns. All on easy slopes with constant accompaniment so you feel safe from the start.

  • Use of lifts (carpet to beginner chairlift)
  • Comfortable and controlled wedge turns
  • Management of first descents without fear
  • Constant and personalized accompaniment
Intermediate

Improve Your Technique and Gain Autonomy

If you've already skied and can handle blue slopes, but feel you repeat mistakes or lack fluidity, this lesson will help you consolidate your technical bases and gain confidence in different conditions.

  • Improvement of balance and outside ski support
  • Refinement of turns: advanced wedge and transition to parallel
  • Work on different terrains and snow types
  • Terrain reading and rhythm management
Advanced

Master Difficult Snow and Your Own Technique

For experienced skiers looking to take their skiing to the next level. We perfect advanced technique and adaptation to challenging conditions.

  • Short turns at high rhythm and carving turns
  • Management of fog, ice and spring snow
  • Skiing in moguls or controlled off-piste
  • Video-analysis and Carv technology available

Frequently Asked Questions about Ski Lessons for Adults

‚ùì Can I start skiing even if I've never done it before and I'm already an adult?

Yes, of course. It's never too late to start! In fact, many of our adult students discover skiing at 40, 50 or even 60 years old.

Our instructors are prepared to teach you from scratch, with a respectful approach, without pressure and totally adapted to your pace. You'll learn to feel comfortable on skis, to brake, turn and enjoy with safety from day one.

‚ùì I've had a fall or negative experience... can you help me regain confidence?
