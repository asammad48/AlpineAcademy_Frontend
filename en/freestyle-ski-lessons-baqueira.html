First Jumps, Switch and Basic Modules

Ideal for riders who ski well in parallel on red slopes and feel curious about freestyle. You'll learn the technical fundamentals: switch (skiing backwards with control), small jumps, ollies, nose/tail press, first rails and easy snowpark modules.

Minimum requirements:

Recommended equipment:

Twin-tip skis, centered bindings, mandatory helmet, back protector and optional protections.

Frequently Asked Questions about Freestyle Lessons in Baqueira

What minimum technical level do I need to start freestyle safely?

To start freestyle safely in Baqueira it's essential that you ski fluently in parallel on red slopes.

This guarantees that you have the necessary control, balance and speed management to move around the snowpark, ski switch, perform stable transitions and manage landings. No previous jumping experience is necessary, but basic alpine skiing mastery is required so you can focus on technical learning without unnecessary risks.

What equipment do I need for my freestyle lessons?

You need twin-tip skis (double tip) with bindings mounted in the center of the ski (optional but advisable), as they allow skiing switch and performing rotations with greater control.

Helmet is mandatory. We also recommend back protector, tailbone protection (butt pad) and specific mask or goggles. If you don't have the equipment, we can advise you in Baqueira to rent or buy what's necessary according to your level and objectives.
