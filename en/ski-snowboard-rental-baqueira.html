Where to rent skis and snowboards in Baqueira?

If you're coming to ski in Baqueira and don't have your own equipment, renting the right gear is key to enjoying the experience to the fullest.

Alpine Ski Academy is a ski and snowboard school not a rental shop, but we offer a complementary and free service: personally advising you to choose the most suitable equipment for your level, your discipline and the resort conditions.

We collaborate with establishments located in strategic and very convenient areas, both in Ruda and Vielha, which have new and updated equipment, adapted to all levels: from beginners to expert skiers on piste, all-mountain, freeride or freestyle.

Free Advisory Service!

When booking your ski lessons or snowboard lessons, if you're interested in this service, we'll send you via WhatsApp a link with: how to get to the recommended shops, schedules, contact information, recommendations according to your level and exclusive details for coming through Alpine Ski Academy.

Our free advisory service

At Alpine Ski Academy we help you choose the ideal equipment for you at no additional cost. This service is included with any booking of our lessons.

For Beginners

We'll tell you what type of skis, boots and protections to request. If you need ski lessons for children or beginner adults, our advice will be very helpful.

For Families

We guide you about helmets, sizing and safety so that group or private ski lessons for children are a success.

For Experts

We help you choose between piste skis, all-mountain, freeride or freestyle, according to your profile. If you're interested in Carv technology, the equipment is also key.

Just tell us your height, level and lesson type, and we'll take care of telling you what type of equipment suits you best.

Because a good lesson starts with good equipment selection.

Recommended equipment types according to your profile

We detail the type of equipment we usually recommend for each skier profile, ensuring you optimize your learning and enjoyment on the slopes.

Beginner (children and adult beginners)

  • Soft skis, short radius and narrow waist
  • Easy to turn, not demanding
  • Comfortable boots, without excessive stiffness
  • Helmet mandatory
  • For children: skis shorter than height and back protectors

Intermediate level (piste and technical improvement)

  • Skis with greater reactivity, but controllable
  • Medium radius, waist between 70 and 80 mm
  • Boots with medium stiffness adapted to weight
  • Poles appropriate to height

Freeride / All-Mountain

  • Longer skis, with greater flotation
  • Wide waist (90+ mm)
  • Solid and stable structure
  • Firm boots with good transmission

Technical lessons / high level on piste

  • High-end or specific piste skis
  • Radius adapted to discipline (slalom, giant...)
  • Rigid boot, well adjusted
  • Equipment check recommended before lesson

Freestyle

  • Twin tip skis (preferably center-mounted bindings)
  • Medium/wide waist and moderate flex
  • Boots with good cushioning
  • Helmet, back protection and knee pads optional

How is rental organized?

1

When to pick up equipment?

Whenever possible, we recommend picking up equipment the day before, especially if your lesson is early. This allows you to avoid rush, try boots calmly and make adjustments if necessary.

2

How much time is needed?

If you have to pick it up the same day, we recommend arriving at least 45 minutes before your lesson. Shops open at 8:30 am, which gives you enough time to organize everything if your ski or snowboard lesson is at 9:30.

3

Where are the shops?

The shops we collaborate with are located in Ruda, Salardú and Vielha, in very accessible areas. We'll send you a direct link via WhatsApp when confirming your booking with addresses, schedules and how to get there from your accommodation.

4

What should you bring or know?

We help you know what equipment you need according to your level. The shops will have it prepared (as much as possible) and adjusted to your profile. And you'll receive exclusive details for coming through Alpine Ski Academy.

Frequently asked questions about equipment rental

Is equipment rental included in the lesson price?

No. Equipment rental is an external service. At Alpine Ski Academy we help you manage it easily and at no additional cost, but rental payment is made directly at the shop. In return, you'll receive exclusive details for coming through us.

Can I rent equipment on the same day as the lesson?

Yes, although we recommend picking it up the day before if your lesson starts early. Shops open at 8:30 am, so if you arrive with enough time (about 45 minutes before the lesson), you can also manage it in the morning without problem.

Need help with your equipment?

Write to us when making your booking and we'll send you everything you need: shop address, what to request, when to pick it up and how to get your exclusive details.
