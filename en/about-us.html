Who We Are

At Alpine Ski Academy we are ski and snowboard instructors in Baqueira with a clear mission: to help you grow on the snow, whatever your level.

Our team consists of certified professionals with years of experience in teaching alpine skiing and snowboarding technique. But beyond qualifications, what defines us is our passion for teaching, close treatment and a methodology adapted to each person.

We work in one of the most privileged environments in the Pyrenees: the Baqueira Beret resort, where we teach dynamic and safe personalized classes, with a progressive and results-oriented approach.

If you're looking for a ski or snowboard instructor in Baqueira who combines technical knowledge, real experience and an authentic connection with the mountains, you're in the right place.

Our History on the Snow

As skiing and snowboarding professionals, we've been teaching in Baqueira since 2001. During this time, we've witnessed the evolution of the resort from within and trained in the best ski schools in the Aran Valley, where we grew as skiers and as people.

Many of us started by taking lessons from great snow references. Years later, that vocation led us to become trainers, first in other academies and finally creating our own project: Alpine Ski Academy.

This school was born from experience, commitment to technical teaching and the desire to offer truly personalized support.

Baqueira is not just our workplace: it's our home, our starting point and the environment that has shaped our way of understanding the mountains.

Philosophy, Vision and Methodology

At Alpine Ski Academy we believe that every person has a unique way of learning, which is why we design classes that adapt to the pace, level and objectives of each student.

Our philosophy is based on respect for individual processes, safe progression and technique as a tool to better enjoy the mountains. We apply progressive teaching, based on active observation, constant feedback and the use of technical resources adapted to each learning phase. We work with children starting out, adults seeking confidence, and technical skiers who want to perfect their level or prepare for official tests.

We don't just "give classes": we train conscious, autonomous and motivated skiers. We prioritize experiential learning, understanding movement and real-time decision making.

Our approach is close, technical and human. We get involved in each learning process as if it were our own. Teaching is not just our profession: it's our commitment to those who, like us, have made snow part of their life.

Our Team of Instructors

Our team consists of certified alpine skiing and snowboard instructors, with extensive teaching experience and a solid technical foundation. We all have official certifications recognized by the Spanish and international sports education system (TD1, TD2, TD3), and we also work as trainers of sports technicians in centers such as SAFE Formación, where we teach the TD1 and TD2 cycles, as well as the Ski Mastery subject at INEFC (4th year).

Frequently Asked Questions

What should you consider when choosing the best ski instructor in Baqueira?

Choosing your ski instructor in Baqueira well can make the difference between a more or less fun experience... and one that truly helps you progress, gain confidence and enjoy the mountains more.

Although official qualification (TD1, TD2 or TD3) is fundamental — as it guarantees technical knowledge, safety and pedagogy — it's not the only factor that matters. A good instructor should also have experience in the terrain, know how to read the resort conditions, adapt to weather, equipment and, above all, to the student. Empathy, clarity in communication, real commitment to learning and the ability to transmit motivation are essential qualities.

Ultimately, the best instructor isn't just the one who knows the most, but the one who teaches, supports and adapts best to each person. That's precisely what we look for in our team at Alpine Ski Academy: qualifications, yes, but also humanity, vocation and dedication.

How to choose the best ski school in Baqueira?

Choosing the best ski school in Baqueira doesn't depend only on the name or the size of the structure. There are several key factors that will help you make the right decision: that their instructors are registered with official organizations (ROPEC) and have their insurance in order.

  • That it has officially certified instructors
  • That it offers personalized and real student follow-up
  • That it works with technology applied to learning (like Carv or video analysis)
  • That it has consolidated experience in the resort
  • That it has verified opinions and real testimonials

Often, opting for a small and specialized school is a great advantage: direct attention, fluid communication, more cared-for classes and a team that truly knows each student.

At Alpine Ski Academy we believe in that model: technical and rigorous teaching, but close, with real people supporting other people in their learning on the snow.
