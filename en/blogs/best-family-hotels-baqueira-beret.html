The Best Family Hotels and Apartments in the Aran Valley: Our Recommendations for the 25-26 Season

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Best Family Hotels in Baqueira Beret

Editor's note: At Alpine Ski Academy, we review and update our recommendations year after year to ensure our guide is always the most accurate and relevant for your trip. This is our selection for the 25-26 season, with all the latest news and updates.

Introduction: Your Expert Ski and Accommodation Guide

At Alpine Ski Academy, we know that your skiing experience doesn't end on the slopes. The place where you rest and recharge is key to making the most of your vacation in Baqueira Beret. Over the years, we have met the best hospitality professionals in the Aran Valley and have carefully selected the hotels and apartments that best suit families and groups of skiers.

That's why, as your guides and instructors on the snow, we share with you a selection of accommodations with a family atmosphere, guaranteed quality, and the proximity you need to make the most of every minute in the mountains. These are hotels and apartments run by local people, who live in the Aran Valley and will make you feel at home, offering you an authentic and personal experience. If you haven't booked your lessons yet, discover our ski and snowboard lesson offerings and book your experience.

1. Hotels and Apartments for Mountain and Tradition Lovers

These accommodations combine the rustic charm of the Valley with all modern comforts.

Hotel Seixes de Bagergue

Bagergue

Tranquility and Stunning Views

Located in the highest village of the Aran Valley, Hotel Seixes offers rooms with stunning views of the mountains and Aneto. Its cozy atmosphere makes it a perfect choice for those looking to combine skiing, mountains, and tranquility.

Instructor's tip: If you stay here, mention that you come from Alpine Ski Academy so they'll have a very special treat for you.

Discover Hotel Seixes de Bagergue

Hotel Deth País in Salardú

Salardú

Close to Slopes and Local Cuisine

This family hotel offers comfortable rooms and high-quality services, located near the ski slopes of Baqueira Beret. The rooms are spacious and the hotel has a local cuisine restaurant.

Learn about Hotel Deth País

Garos Ostau

Aran Valley

Hospitality and Family Atmosphere

This charming accommodation offers cozy rooms and personalized services. It is known for its hospitality and family atmosphere, with rustic-style rooms and views of the Aran Valley.

Instructor's tip: When contacting Alicia, mention that you come recommended by Alpine Ski Academy and you'll receive even better service.

Visit Garos Ostau

2. Apartments for Families and Groups

If you're traveling with your loved ones or a group of friends, an apartment offers the space and freedom you need.

Apartamento Vista Bagergue

Bagergue

The Perfect Place for Large Families

Enjoy one of the best views of the Aran Valley in this cozy apartment. Perfect for large families or groups, it has 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and a spacious living room with fireplace to relax after a day of skiing.

Instructor's tip: Don't forget to mention that you come from Alpine Ski Academy to get a 5% discount on your accommodation!

View Apartamento Vista Bagergue

Es De Pau Casa Rural

Aran Valley

Authentic High Mountain Experience

This rural house accommodates up to 6 people, with a fully equipped kitchen, fireplace, and a patio with barbecue to enjoy the outdoors and nature.

Book your stay at Es De Pau Casa Rural

3. Hotels with Snow Connection: Ski Straight from the Door!

These accommodations are designed for skiers and offer maximum comfort to reach the slopes.

SNö Mont Romies Hotel & Apartaments

Baqueira Beret

Direct Access to the Slopes

Located in a beautiful natural setting, this hotel offers spacious apartments and direct access to the slopes of Baqueira Beret. It has children's play areas and shuttle service to the resort.

Why Choose Our Recommended Accommodations?

  • Proximity to Slopes: All are strategically located so you can make the most of your time in the snow.
  • Family Environments: Most have specific services for families, ensuring a comfortable and fun stay for everyone.
  • Quality and Comfort: They have been selected for their quality and the amenities they offer.
  • Support for Local Businesses: By choosing these accommodations, you support local professionals who will make you feel like part of their family. These businesses are the heart of the Aran Valley.

Frequently Asked Questions about Hotels in Baqueira Beret

What family hotels do you recommend near the slopes of Baqueira Beret?

At Alpine Ski Academy we recommend hotels managed by local hoteliers such as Hotel Seixes in Bagergue, Garos Ostau and Hotel Deth País in Salardú, all very close to the slopes and with an authentic family atmosphere. For those who prefer more independence, we highlight Apartamento Vista Bagergue and Es De Pau Casa Rural, ideal for families and groups who want to cook and have more space during their stay.

Is it better to stay in a hotel or apartment in the Aran Valley?

It depends on your travel plan. Hotels offer comfort, extra services and personalized attention that makes you feel at home. Apartments are a great option for families or groups seeking flexibility, additional space and the possibility of preparing their own meals.
