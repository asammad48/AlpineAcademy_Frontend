Frequently Asked Questions about Your Snowboard Lessons

What equipment do I need and what protections are essential for learning snowboarding?

For your snowboard lessons, it is essential to bring a board, boots and bindings suitable for your level. If you don't have your own equipment, you can rent it at the specialized shops in Baqueira Beret.

As an experienced instructor, my clear safety recommendation is the following: The helmet is mandatory for everyone. Additionally, specific snowboard goggles or mask are highly recommended for optimal vision and eye protection.

For your first falls, which are an inevitable part of learning, we strongly advise specific protections: a back protector (mandatory for children!) and crash pants with tailbone protection, essential to avoid impacts when sitting on the ground and also while riding. Your safety is our priority so you can enjoy every descent to the maximum!

What is the recommended age to start learning snowboarding?

At Alpine Ski Academy, based on our experience and methodology, we consider that the ideal age for children to start their snowboard lessons is from 5-6 years old.

At this age, children have usually developed the coordination and balance necessary to approach the fundamentals of snowboarding in a fun and progressive way, guaranteeing safe and effective learning.

Our team of professionals will adapt each lesson to their pace and, as always, prioritizing their safety with the appropriate equipment!
