Frequently Asked Questions about Carv Usage

❓ Do I need to have my own Carv for this lesson?

No. We provide the Carv sensor during the lesson - we install it directly in your boots, which are compatible without exception.

And if you already have your own Carv, the experience will be even more complete: we'll work with your previous data and configure the settings to optimize each analysis.

❓ What difference does Carv make compared to a traditional technical lesson?

Carv provides data that no human eye can register in real time.

During the lesson, we will measure and analyze with you variables such as: – Turn parallelism and shape – Early edging and angle amount – Progressive inclination – Symmetry between left and right turns – Anteroposterior balance – G-forces generated in each turn

This information, interpreted by our Carv expert instructors, becomes immediate feedback and personalized exercises that allow you to improve with unprecedented precision.
