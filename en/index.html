We Solve Your Key Questions for Skiing in Baqueira

We know that planning your snow getaway can generate questions. Here we answer some of the most important ones:

I'm not sure about my ski/snowboard level, how do I choose the right lesson?

Don't worry! Our expert team will help you. Contact us, tell us about your previous experience or send us a video of your last ski session to our WhatsApp (+34 669 911 342). With that information, we'll recommend the ideal lesson in Baqueira for you to make the most of from the first minute, ensuring that the choice is the most appropriate for your progression.

What is your cancellation policy for ski lessons?

We understand that unforeseen events may arise. You can cancel your lessons at no cost if you notify us at least 24 hours in advance. Likewise, if lessons cannot be taught due to total station closure or weather safety reasons, we will proceed with the refund or look for an alternative. Check our complete cancellation policy here.

Is it mandatory to wear a helmet for skiing in Baqueira or in your lessons?

Although it is not legally required for adults on slopes, at Alpine Ski Academy we strongly recommend it for everyone. For children, we require it, in addition to recommending back protectors. In freestyle or freeride lessons, the helmet is equally mandatory. Safety in the snow is our top priority. Ski with your head, ski safely!

Do you offer discounts or special promotions on ski lessons in Baqueira?

At Alpine Ski Academy, we bet on a transparent pricing policy and exceptional value. Our rates are the result of a careful annual market study, positioning us competitively. We offer premium quality at a fair price, without increases due to high demand. If you book lessons of 3-4 hours or full day, the price per hour improves slightly. Additionally, afternoon lessons have a reduction of €5/hour.
