For What Age and Level Are These Lessons?

At Alpine Ski Academy we offer private ski lessons for children from 3 years old through adolescence. Each stage of child development requires a different approach, which is why our lessons precisely adapt to each student's level, maturity, energy and pace.

3 Years

Initiation with Play, Safety and Own Pace

The main objective is for the child to enjoy, feel safe and start sliding with joy. We don't seek a formal lesson, but a positive experience that awakens their curiosity for snow.

  • Flexible duration: 1-2 hours
  • Pace set by the child
  • Adaptation to family routines
  • Possibility of hotel pick-up
4-6 Years

Safety, Play and Technical Progress

Children show greater autonomy and motor coordination. We continue using play as a central tool, but with focus on balance, braking and basic wedge turns.

  • Soft and safe areas
  • Introduction of wedges and back protectors
  • Development of balance and control
  • Areas: Carreretes, Cabana, beginners
7-10 Years

Technique, Confidence and Autonomy

Strengthening of technique: evolved wedge turns, parallel initiation, speed control and autonomy with simple lifts.

  • Combination of technique and play
  • Gentle challenges in new areas
  • Introduction to Fun Park
  • Development of autonomy
10+ Years

Consolidation and New Challenges

We work on more advanced technical objectives according to the student's level: complex turns, basic carving, adaptation to different snow types and snowpark.

  • Dynamic and challenging approach
  • Advanced technique
  • Era Marmota Snowpark
  • Reinforcement of autonomy

What Does Your Child Need for Their First Lesson?

At Alpine Ski Academy we take care of making everything easy for you and your child. For the experience to be comfortable, safe and positive from the first moment, we recommend coming with appropriate equipment and well-chosen clothing.

Clothing and Protection

  • ✅ Thermal base layer
  • ✅ Fleece or mid layer
  • ✅ Waterproof pants and jacket
  • ✅ Warm and waterproof gloves
  • ✅ Hat or buff to wear under helmet
  • ✅ Ski goggles or sunglasses
  • ✅ Sunscreen and lip protection

Technical Equipment

  • ✅ Children's skis with bindings
  • ✅ Comfortable boots, well adjusted
  • ✅ Helmet mandatory
  • ✅ Back protector recommended
  • ✅ Wedges for first times
  • ✅ Poles (if instructor indicates)

What if something is missing? Don't worry. If you don't have wedges or need sunscreen, we bring these support materials. The rest of the equipment must be brought by the student or you can easily rent it.

Frequently Asked Questions about Kids Ski Lessons

What minimum age do you recommend to start with private ski lessons?

We recommend starting from 3 years old and always when the child walks confidently and no longer uses diapers.

At these ages, skiing should be a positive and respectful experience understanding each child and family's rhythms and preferences. That's why our lessons fully adapt to their needs with flexible duration, soft and safe areas, play as main axis and instructors specialized in children's teaching.

What happens if my child doesn't want to continue or gets overwhelmed during the lesson?

It's completely normal for some children to feel insecure or simply have a bad day.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we never force situations. Our instructors are trained to manage emotions with empathy, patience and play. If the child needs to stop, rest or simply observe for a while, we respect it. We always prioritize their emotional well-being.
