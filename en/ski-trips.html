A new dimension of skiing

After so many seasons shared in Baqueira, we know that skiing, for many of you, goes far beyond being a sport. It's disconnection. It's intensity. It's technique, landscape, emotion, health, family. It's a way of living winter through experiences that nourish body and mind, that are remembered for a lifetime.

That's why Alpine Ski Academy Trips was born: a project designed for those who want to continue exploring the world through skiing, with the accompaniment of the teachers you already know, in the places you've always dreamed of.

Each season we will choose an iconic destination to propose a complete adventure: technical, cultural, scenic and human. Places where the alpine skiing culture is breathed in every village, every lift, every descent. Legendary resorts in the French, Swiss, Italian or Austrian Alps. Infinite domains that combine history, elegance, gastronomy, tradition and breathtaking landscapes.

The 2025-2026 season we will ski in St. Moritz, one of the most prestigious destinations on the planet. An experience that is not just technical: it's a privilege. And it's open both to those who have grown with our school in Baqueira and to those who want to join this new way of traveling and skiing. The organized ski trips with Alpine Ski Academy are the perfect opportunity to experience skiing in dream destinations.

Destination 2025: St. Moritz (Switzerland)

In April 2025 we begin our first great adventure outside Baqueira with a destination worthy of the occasion: St. Moritz, in the heart of the Swiss Alps.

Why St. Moritz?

  • ✅ More than 300 sunny days per year: one of the most privileged climates in the Alps
  • ✅ Three connected resorts: Corviglia, Diavolezza and Corvatsch
  • ✅ A spectacular environment, with slopes around a mythical lake
  • ✅ Varied orientations that allow us to choose the best face each day according to conditions
  • ✅ Glacier and altitude for guaranteed snow in April
  • ✅ State-of-the-art lifts and wide, long and well-maintained slopes
  • ✅ A combination of luxury, tradition and authentic alpine culture

And above all, because we believe it's a destination that perfectly fits the natural evolution of those who have skied with us in Baqueira for years and are ready to take the next step.

Frequently asked questions about ski trips

Do I need to have a high level to participate?

You don't need to be an expert skier, but we recommend having a solid intermediate level (red runs with autonomy).

If you have doubts, consult us and we'll guide you.

Can I come without booking lessons?

Lessons are included in the trip and do not represent an additional cost.

If you decide not to take advantage of them, the price remains the same, as it is part of the package. Still, we encourage you to participate: they are adapted, technical and very enriching sessions.

Are you ready to travel with us?

This is not just any trip. It's the opportunity to experience skiing like never before: in a legendary destination, with the comfort of professional organization and the accompaniment of the teachers you already trust.
