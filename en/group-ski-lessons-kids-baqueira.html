Group Ski Lessons for Kids in Baqueira

Safety, Play and Real Learning with Our Certified Instructors

At Alpine Ski Academy we know that children learn better when they have fun, feel safe and share the experience with others their age. That's why our group lessons for children in Baqueira combine technique, motivation and play in a controlled and cheerful environment.

We form small homogeneous groups by age and level, with morning and afternoon schedules and certified instructors with experience in children's pedagogy. Each class is designed so your child progresses naturally, with confidence and always with a smile. Our Little Skiers in Action

Pedagogical Methodology in Our Group Children's Ski Lessons

Group lessons for children require a specific methodology. At Alpine Ski Academy we don't just teach skiing: we accompany each stage of child development with games, adapted technique and lots of affection.

Small Groups by Age and Level

We form small groups (between 3 and 7 children) with classmates of similar age and level. This allows for a common pace, better communication and a more effective class.

Games and Integrated Learning

Through playful dynamics, children work without realizing it on balance, control, orientation and autonomy. Each class is a new adventure.

Safety and Constant Supervision

Our instructors are not only technical experts: they are trained to care for, guide and constantly observe. Emotional and physical safety is our priority.

For more information about our lessons, contact us!

How Group Lessons for Kids Work in Baqueira

Exclusive Meeting Point

All children's lessons start and end next to the Bosque chairlift (1800) in Baqueira. It's a protected and accessible area where parents can accompany them on arrival and see them start the class calmly.

Adapted Duration and Pace

Lessons have a standard duration of 3 hours, both morning and afternoon. During that time, we alternate technical segments with playful dynamics, short breaks and adaptation to the group's physical and emotional state.

Frequently Asked Questions

From what age can they join a standard ski group?

Standard group lessons are for children from 4 years old, as long as they already do wedge turns or have previous experience.

If your child is 3 to 5 years old and doesn't yet turn, we offer a more personalized alternative with two instructors for a very small group. Check availability via WhatsApp for this children's ski initiation option.

How do we ensure the children's ski group is well formed?

Before the lesson, we assess each child's level and, if possible, we use a short video sent via WhatsApp. This guarantees that the group is homogeneous in age and level, which favors pace, safety and enjoyment. For any questions, don't hesitate to contact us.
