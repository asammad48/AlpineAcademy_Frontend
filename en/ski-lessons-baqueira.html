At Alpine Ski Academy we offer one of the most complete and personalized proposals for ski lessons in Baqueira Beret, the most iconic resort in the Pyrenees. Whether it's your first time in the snow or you want to perfect your technique with precision, here you'll find a lesson type designed for you.

Our goal is for you to enjoy learning, the landscape and each descent with maximum safety and confidence. Each lesson is designed so you progress visibly, guided by certified instructors with extensive experience and passion for teaching.

🧭 A School That Adapts to You

We know no two skiers are alike. That's why we adapt each session to your level, your pace and your personal objectives.

Our methodology combines personalized attention, progressive exercises, and constant technical feedback to help you advance effectively, without losing fun or motivation.

At Alpine Ski Academy we understand that skiing is not just technique: it's also emotion, balance and connection with the mountains. Each lesson becomes a complete experience, where learning flows naturally.

🎿 Types of Ski Lessons in Baqueira

In Baqueira Beret, the environment has everything: wide slopes for beginners, technical areas for intermediates, demanding itineraries for experts and safe spaces for children.

That's why our lessons are organized in different types, always with small groups and close attention.

Private Ski Lessons

Private lessons are the ideal option if you're looking to progress quickly and enjoy 100% personalized treatment.

Your instructor analyzes your initial level and designs a session adapted to your objectives - whether improving parallel turns, perfecting short turns, learning carving or facing your first descents in powder snow.

Available types:
  • Adults (all levels, from initiation to expert)
  • Kids and youth (fun and safe learning)
  • Families (joint lessons to share progress)
  • Carv technology, with sensors that record your performance
  • Freestyle, to master the park with control and creativity
  • Freeride, for those seeking freedom off-piste with safety
Prices and Duration

👉 Approximate price: from 65 €/hour (1 student), +5 €/hour per additional person. We recommend sessions of 2 to 3 consecutive hours.

Group Ski Lessons

If you prefer learning in company, our group lessons in Baqueira Beret combine the energy of collective work with individual technical follow-up.

Groups are homogeneous and small (between 3 and 8 students), which allows maintaining close attention and constant progression.

Available options:
  • Adults Group (by levels: initiation, intermediate, advanced)
  • Kids Group (playful, safe and motivating methodology)
  • Companies and groups (team building on skis)

Each day takes place in a relaxed and motivating atmosphere, where the priority is enjoying learning.

👉 Approximate price: 21 €/hour per student, in courses of 3 or 4 hours daily.

🌨️ Learning to Ski in Baqueira: Much More Than a Lesson

Choosing Baqueira Beret to learn to ski means discovering a privileged environment, with more than 160 km of slopes and first-class services.

As a local school, we know every corner of the resort: the best areas for beginners, the ideal sectors for practicing technique and the most beautiful sections to enjoy the landscape.

Additionally, we help you organize everything surrounding your snow experience:

  • Advice on ski and snowboard equipment rental
  • Tips on restaurants and services at the resort
  • Information about ski passes, access and beginner areas
  • Coordination of photo sessions and après-ski activities

Everything designed so you experience your stay in Baqueira in the most comfortable, complete and memorable way possible.

📅 First Time in Baqueira or Not Sure Which Lesson to Choose?

If you're not sure where to start, write to us via WhatsApp or through the contact form.

We'll be happy to guide you and recommend the most suitable option according to your level, your objectives and the type of experience you desire.

Our team is available every day during the season, and can assist you in Spanish, English, French, Catalan and Portuguese.
