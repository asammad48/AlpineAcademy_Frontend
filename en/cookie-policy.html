1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on the user's device (computer, tablet or mobile) when browsing the Internet.

Their main function is to remember information about the visit, such as language preferences, login or display settings, to improve the user experience.

2. Types of cookies used

The Alpine Ski Academy website uses first-party and third-party cookies for technical, analytical and advertising purposes.

The categories are detailed below:

a) Technical or necessary cookies

They allow navigation and use of the different options or services offered by the website, such as traffic control or session identification.

Example: saving the selected language or maintaining the logged-in session.

(These cookies do not require user consent.)

b) Analysis or measurement cookies

They allow quantifying the number of users and analyzing the use they make of the website to improve its content and services.

Example: Google Analytics cookies, Meta Pixel or Matomo.

c) Personalization cookies

They allow remembering user preferences (for example, language, region or device type) to offer a tailored experience.

d) Advertising or marketing cookies

They allow the management of advertising spaces based on user behavior and browsing habits, in order to show relevant and personalized advertising.

3. Third-party cookies

Alpine Ski Academy may install third-party cookies associated with external services, such as:

  • Google Analytics: analysis of traffic and user behavior on the website.
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram Pixel): campaign measurement and remarketing.
  • YouTube / Vimeo: video integration in website sections.
  • TripAdvisor / Bing / Google Maps: external functionalities related to location, reviews or online reputation.

These providers may process data according to their own policies, which we recommend consulting:

  • Google Policy
  • Meta Policy
  • TripAdvisor Policy

4. Cookie management and configuration

The user can allow, block or delete cookies installed on their device through the configuration of the browser used.

The following links explain how to do this in the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Configure cookies in Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox: Configure cookies in Firefox
  • Safari: Configure cookies in Safari
  • Microsoft Edge: Configure cookies in Edge

You can also configure or withdraw your consent from our cookie settings panel accessible in the website's pop-up notice (bottom banner).

5. Consent

When the user first accesses the Alpine Ski Academy website, a cookie notice is displayed that allows accepting or configuring the categories they wish to enable.

By continuing to browse or clicking "Accept all", you expressly consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

6. Cookie policy updates

Alpine Ski Academy may modify this policy to adapt to future regulatory requirements or changes in the cookie configuration used.

We recommend periodically reviewing this section to stay informed about its use.
