Do you want to learn to ski or perfect your technique without being alone on the slopes? Alpine Ski Academy's group lessons for adults are an excellent alternative to private lessons: technical progress, relaxed atmosphere and a more affordable price, all with certified instructors and dynamics adapted to your level.

We organize homogeneous groups by level, in morning and afternoon schedules, with a maximum of 8 students per class. It's a perfect opportunity to meet other skiers, improve your skiing and enjoy more each day in Baqueira.

Adults Groups in Action

Advantages of Our Group Lessons for Adults

Group lessons for adults are designed so you can learn or improve with safety, good technique and in a motivating environment. It doesn't matter if it's your first time or if you've skied before: we group you by level so you can make the most of the experience.

Technical Learning in Group

Each class works on a clear progression: posture, turns, speed control, supports and terrain adaptation. Although you're in a group, the instructor corrects individually and gives precise technical feedback.

Homogeneous Level and Common Pace

We group by level and experience, so you feel comfortable, progress at a good pace and don't get frustrated. You can send us a short video if you have doubts about your ideal group.

Alternative to Private Lessons

A more economical option, but with the same technical requirements. Ideal for those who want to improve without needing exclusive attention or simply enjoy learning more in a group. For other options, check our private ski lessons for adults.

Morning and Afternoon Groups

Choose the time slot that best fits your day:

  • Morning: 9:30–12:30 h
  • Afternoon: 13:00–16:00 h

The afternoon is ideal if you want to ski with less crowds and take advantage of the reduced pass.

How Group Ski Lessons for Adults Work

We want you to feel comfortable from the first minute. Here we explain how our adult lessons are organized: from the meeting point to group formation and available schedules.

Meeting Point

Classes start and end at a point defined according to the group's level and station conditions (1800, 1500 or Era Cabana).

Group Formation

Groups by level (beginner, intermediate or advanced) with maximum 8 students. Minimum 4 people to open a group.

Previous Evaluation

If you have doubts about your level, you can send us a short video via WhatsApp to assign you to the most suitable group.

Frequently Asked Questions about Group Ski Lessons for Adults

Can I sign up alone or do I have to come with a formed group?

You can come alone without problem. In fact, most adult students sign up individually. We take care of assigning you to the most suitable group by level, schedule and learning pace.

What happens if a group doesn't form in the time slot I choose?

For an adult group to form, we need a minimum of 4 participants. Sometimes, especially in low season or for very specific levels, it can be difficult to reach this number. If this happens in the time slot or level you've chosen, don't worry, we'll always look for the best solution so you can enjoy the snow and continue learning.

We'll propose real and flexible alternatives:

  • Another compatible group: We'll look for a group with a similar level that fits your profile in a nearby schedule.
  • Reduced group with adapted rate: If there are 2 or 3 participants interested in the same time slot and level, we can offer the option of forming a reduced group, adjusting the rate for you.
  • Collaboration with partner schools: If none of the previous options fit, we'll help you find a trusted school in Baqueira that we collaborate with, where there is a group that fits your needs.

Our goal is that you can always ski and learn, without complications. Check our rates and prices for ski and snowboard lessons in Baqueira for more information.
