How much does a ski lesson in Baqueira cost?

When looking for the price of a ski lesson in Baqueira, it's easy to focus only on duration or number of students. But there's much more at stake: level, type of lesson, date, days, and even the technology we apply.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we commit to a personalized, professional and high-quality teaching model, and this is reflected in each of our rates. Our lessons are designed for those who value real follow-up, refined technique and a carefully crafted experience from start to finish.

Here we offer you a clear view of our rates and everything they include. If you have questions or special needs, our team will be happy to help you choose the best option for you, your children or your group.

We want to offer you the best experience-price ratio in the sector!

How We Set Our Rates

Each season, our rates are updated after a comprehensive market analysis and review of the more than 20 schools operating in Baqueira.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we understand that the price of a ski lesson should be in line with the real value the student receives: professional, personalized teaching, attention to detail and a firm commitment to your learning.

That's why we set our rates always seeking a coherent relationship between what we offer and what you get.

Frequently Asked Questions about Prices

Do lessons include equipment or ski pass?

No. Our rates cover exclusively the ski or snowboard teaching service. Equipment rental and ski pass are not included.

However, we advise you free of charge with recommended shops and options so you can get what you need easily.

Do prices change according to the season?

No. At Alpine Ski Academy we keep our rates stable throughout the season, without surcharges on key dates like Christmas or Easter.

This way you can plan your ski lessons in Baqueira without surprises or last-minute variations.
