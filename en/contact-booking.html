You can contact us directly via WhatsApp or complete our form. We'll respond as soon as possible, with personalized attention and clear recommendations that fit your needs and level.

What Information to Include for a Quick Response?

To provide you with the most useful and accurate response possible, we recommend including the following details in your inquiry:

Desired date(s)

Help us verify availability for the dates you have in mind.

Number of people

Are you coming alone, as a couple, with friends or with family? This helps us organize the perfect lesson.

Type of lesson

Are you looking for private ski lessons like freestyle, freeride or carve, snowboard lessons, or group lessons?

Skiing level or previous experience

This helps us place you in the appropriate group or private lesson to maximize your learning.

Preferred schedule

Do you prefer morning or afternoon lessons? Your preference helps us organize schedules.

Service Hours and Season

No matter when you contact us, we're always available to help you!

During ski season (winter)

We attend every day from 7:30 to 23:30 h.

Lesson hours on slopes

During skiing hours (9:00-17:00) we might take a bit longer to respond if we're in class (we're instructors and ski every day) but rest assured we'll respond to email or WhatsApp within one or two hours.

Off-season

We also respond via WhatsApp and email, though less frequently. Prepare your next ski vacation in advance!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We've compiled some of the most common questions to help you quickly:

How far in advance should I book?

The sooner you book, the better! Especially during holidays, long weekends and high seasons. Some time slots and lesson types fill up weeks in advance.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to guarantee availability, especially if you have specific dates in mind.

Can I book several lessons at once?

Yes, of course. We'll advise you to organize personalized planning by days and levels, ensuring continuous progress.

In fact, we recommend booking several consecutive lessons to maximize your learning and make the most of your stay in Baqueira.

Where do we respond from?

We're strategically located in Baqueira 1500, a ski school with direct presence on the slopes.

This allows us to offer you real and updated knowledge of snow conditions, slopes and daily conditions.
